Egyptian Toast Recipe

Description

The Perfect mix of toast and egg

Ingridients

  • 1 Tablespoon of Butter
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 1 Slice of Bread
  • Salt and Pepper

Recipe

  1. Toast bread slice until brown, spread a light coat of butter on both sides

  2. Cut a 1 inch hole in middle of toast (Save the center for future dipping)

  3. Fry your egg on medium heat. Once the bottom has rendered, place hole of toast on top of egg yolk

  4. Flip when egg and toast have merged together. Continue cooking to liking.
